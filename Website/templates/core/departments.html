Departments

Explore the various departments and teams dedicated to supporting individuals with bipolar disorder and their families.

Psychiatry Department

Specializes in diagnosing and treating mental health disorders, including bipolar disorder.

Clinical Psychology Department

Provides psychological evaluation and therapy for mental health conditions, including bipolar disorder.

Neuropsychiatry Department

Integrates psychiatry and neurology to address complex mental health issues with neurological components.

Pharmacy Department

Manages medication therapy and provides pharmaceutical care for patients with bipolar disorder.

Social Work Department

Provides support services and counseling for patients and families dealing with mental health issues.

Crisis Intervention Unit

Provides immediate support and stabilization for individuals in acute mental health crises.

Research and Development Department

Conducts research on new treatments and interventions for mental health disorders, including bipolar disorder.
